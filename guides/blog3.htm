How to play Flash Games Offline in 2023

With Adobe ending its Flash support on modern browsers back in late 2020, you may want to try your old favorite Flash games for fun or a nostalgia fest. Here in this guide, I will show you how to achieve that!

We will be using Flashpoint Archive (formerly known as BlueMaxima's Flashpoint), a community-driven open-source project that aims to archive all Flash games & animations.

Installation

First, you will need to head to BlueMaxima's Flashpoint, then click on Download. It will then ask which version you wish to download. If you are having trouble choosing one, hereâ€™s the difference:

Flashpoint 12 Infinity is for casual users who want to just experience a few games or are tight on disk space. You can freely download games whenever you want to play, even offline.

Flashpoint 12 Ultimate is for people who just want to keep all games for some reason; games are already preinstalled from the get-go.

Once you have downloaded the version you want, start up the setup, click on Install Flashpoint, and let it do its thing.
